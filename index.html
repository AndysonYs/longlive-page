Short Window Attention & Frame Sink

Comparison in a 20s generated video of long window attention (21 local latent frames), short-window attention (9 local), and short-window + frame-sink (6 local + 3 sink).
Shorter windows boost efficiency but weaken long-range consistency; adding a frame-sink restores consistency while keeping the efficiency gains.
